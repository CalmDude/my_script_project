# Backtest Results

This folder contains historical backtest analysis results and strategy performance metrics.

## Files

### October-December 2024 Analysis
- **backtest_results_oct_dec_2024.csv** - Detailed trade-by-trade results (105 trades, 13 weeks)
- **backtest_summary_oct_dec_2024.csv** - Summary metrics by quality, rank, and filters

### Strategy 1 Analysis (5 Years: 2021-2025)
- **backtest_strategy1_results.csv** - Empty (filters found to be impossible)
- **backtest_strategy1_summary.csv** - Analysis showing 0 matching trades
- **actual_strategy_combinations.csv** - All tested filter combinations with performance metrics

## Key Findings

### Oct-Dec 2024 (Short Sample)
- Period: 13 weeks during GREEN regime (bull market)
- Total trades: 105
- Notable: Small sample size, regime bias

### 5-Year Historical (2021-2025)
- Period: Full market cycles including 2022 bear market
- Total trades: 1,030
- Best win rate achieved: **42.8%** (vs 70-80% in short sample)
- Conclusion: Short sample was misleading due to bull market bias
